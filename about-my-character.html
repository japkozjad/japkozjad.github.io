    Story


    In a land known as Equestria, there lived a young pegasus whose name is Apply. Apply's story began in the heart of Cloudsdale, a vibrant city in the sky where pegasus ponies ruled the skies. Apply was born to loving parents, who were both skilled and respected members of the weather team, responsible for controlling the weather and maintaining the harmony of their realm.


    From a young age, Apply was a bright and curious pegasus, his coat a soft shade of pale blue and his eyes, one of them was in the color of the clearest summer sky, the second one was in the color of golden sunshine. Yet, despite his natural talents and the support of his family, Apply was a bit apprehensive about venturing beyond the comfortable boundaries of Cloudsdale. The world beyond the clouds was vast and unknown, and Apply's cautious nature made him hesitant to explore it.


    Apply had a deep fascination with music, and he would often spend hours listening to the melodies of the birds, the gentle hum of the wind, and the rhythmic beat of the raindrops. He longed to create his own music, but he was a little afraid of performing it in front of others. The idea of sharing his melodies and facing an audience filled him with trepidation.


    His parents, who had once been just like him, understood his fears and gently encouraged him to take his time. They shared stories of their own youthful dreams and how they had overcome their anxieties to pursue their passions. They assured Apply that the world was not as scary as it might seem and that he had the skills to share his music with others when he felt ready.


    As Apply grew, he practiced his musical talents in private, composing beautiful melodies that resonated with the emotions of those who heard them. Yet, he kept his melodies a well-kept secret, sharing them only with his closest family and friends.


    One day, as Apply gazed down at the breathtaking landscapes below Cloudsdale, he felt a deep longing to share his music with a wider audience. He realized that it was time to overcome his fear of performing in front of others and to take his melodies to the world below.


    With the support of his parents, who believed in his musical gifts, Apply spread his wings and took flight, his heart a mix of excitement and fear. As he left the comfort of Cloudsdale and descended toward the lands below, he knew that the journey ahead would be filled with both challenges and opportunities to share his music. But with the love of his family and his newfound courage, Apply was determined to explore the world, discover its musical wonders, and share his melodies, one harmonious adventure at a time.
