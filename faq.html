Why Japkozjad?

When I was making my second attempt to make a YouTube channel, I was searching for a good nickname which will be original and unique. The idea came from my surname.


The Japko part has been taken from uncorrectly written word "jabłko" (Eng. "an apple").


The zjad part is uncorrectly written word "zjadł" which in Polish means "ate".


Literal translation of my nickname is a person who ate an apple :3

What was your first experience with music? How it started?

My first contact with music was when I was probably 10 years old. My mom was playing her favorite song on a keyboard synthesizer, I wanted to play like her, and I guess that's how it started. 3 years later, I installed the demo version of FL Studio 12 because I wanted to try making my own music. Due to the price of this powerful software, I switched to LMMS (Linux Multimedia Studio) because it was free, and it was a good place to start composing music. Since 2022, I've become an active FL Studio user, and I enjoy seeing how far I've come with my creativity as well as my skills.
